About Me

Hello, I’m Nova Mae P. Sariola, a 3rd-year student pursuing a degree in BSIT-Animation at Bicol University Polangui. My passion lies in creating captivating animations and bringing stories to life through visuals. Whether it’s 2D, 3D, or stop-motion, I love exploring different styles and techniques to improve my craft.

Born on November 19, 2003, I enjoy sketching, watching animated films, and experimenting with new animation software. I aspire to work as a character designer or storyboard artist in the future. I’m excited to contribute to the animation industry and create impactful visual experiences.
